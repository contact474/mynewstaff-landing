## VENUE REQUIREMENTS

### Space
- Single main room, ~1,500 sq ft, capacity 25 seated at tables (not theater)
- Long tables or boardroom style — NOT rows. Everyone on laptops.
- Good natural light + controllable blackout for screens
- Private — no walk-in traffic, no shared space with other events
- Separate breakout / chill space (couches, coffee)
- Kitchen access for catering drop-off

### Tech
- 1 Gbps+ wired internet (backup: mobile hotspot pre-tested)
- Dedicated WiFi, no password sharing with other tenants
- 4K TV or projector with HDMI + USB-C
- Power — 1 outlet per seat minimum (power strips if needed)
- Backup: 2 power strips, 2 HDMI cables, 1 USB-C to HDMI

### Audio/Video
- Lapel mic x 2 (Luka + Los)
- Room speakers
- **RECORD EVERYTHING** — wide-angle camera on tripod + screen recording on host laptop
- Livestream capability (optional — could promote next cohort)
