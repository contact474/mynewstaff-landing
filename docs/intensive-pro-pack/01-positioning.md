# POSITIONING — THE ANGLE

## The one-line pitch
> **"Two and a half days in LA. Walk in with a business. Walk out with an AI machine running it."**

## The anti-pitch (what this is NOT)
- Not a speaker event. No one is clapping for a slide deck.
- Not a "learn AI" workshop. You already know AI is happening.
- Not a guru retreat. No cold plunges, no journaling circles.
- Not another mastermind sales-fest. The mastermind is the back-end, not the product.

## What it IS
A controlled room of 20 operators sitting next to Luka and Los for 3 days while the most advanced AI stack in the game gets installed on their actual business. Everyone leaves with something running — a cold caller, a content engine, a lead system, or all three.

## The founder story (use in posts / DMs / video)
Luka runs MyNewStaff.ai — the AI stack that's quietly powering 7-figure operators across e-commerce, coaching, and agencies. Most of what he's built is not public. The intensive is the first time anyone outside the client list gets their hands on it.

Los has advised high-ticket masterminds paying $35K–$40K/year. He's watched operators struggle to turn AI hype into revenue. The intensive is his answer: strip out the noise, put operators in a room with the stack, and don't let them leave until it's working.

## The enemy (what we're positioning against)
- **AI courses.** 47 hours of video, zero shipped. Dead.
- **$2K/mo "AI marketing agencies"** that churn ChatGPT templates.
- **Mastermind FOMO** where you pay $30K and get a WhatsApp group.
- **Twitter AI gurus** who've never deployed anything.

## The hook lines (rotate in content)
- "If AI isn't running your business by June, you're getting priced out."
- "47 hours of AI courses = 0 shipped systems. 3 days with us = 1 running machine."
- "The operators we work with aren't learning AI. They're weaponizing it."
- "You don't need another mastermind. You need your stack running."
- "We built the machine. Now we're installing it. 20 seats."

## Tone rules
- Confident, not cocky. We've shipped it. We don't have to pretend.
- Direct. No hedging. No "might" or "could help."
- Operator-to-operator. Not coach-to-student.
- Zero AI-speak. No "unlock," "revolutionize," "leverage," "game-changer."
- Short sentences. Punch. Stop.
