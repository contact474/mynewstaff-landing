# DM SCRIPTS — Jeremy Miner NEPQ style

**Rule:** Questions, not pitches. Curiosity, not closing. The page closes them.

---

## DM #1 — Warm List (people who already know you)

**Opener:**
> Hey [Name] — quick one. You still running [their business/role]?

*(Wait for reply. DO NOT paste pitch. Let them engage first.)*

**Follow-up after reply:**
> Cool. Los and I are doing something small in LA May 6–8 — 20 operators, 3 days, we install the actual AI stack we run at MNS on their business. No course, no pitch fest.
>
> Not sure if it's a fit for you right now — are you even thinking about AI in [their business] or is it not really on the radar yet?

**If they bite:**
> Yeah makes sense. If you're open to it I can send you the page — 20 seats, half already spoken for. Want me to send?

**If they want it:** Send `https://www.mynewstaff.ai/intensive`

## DM #3 — Cold (targeted — agency owners, coaches, e-com founders)

**Rule:** Lead with THEIR situation, not our event.

**Opener:**
> Hey [Name] — saw your post on [specific thing]. Genuine question, not a pitch — when you look at your delivery / fulfillment, what's eating most of the hours right now?

*(The trick: ask about their pain, not your solution. 60%+ reply rate.)*

**Follow-up after they share:**
> Makes sense. Reason I ask — that's the exact thing we've been solving for operators with AI at MNS. Not fluff, actual cold callers and content engines running 24/7.
>
> I'm not selling you anything in the DM, but Los and I are running a small hands-on thing in LA May 6–8 where we literally install it on people's businesses. If I sent you the page would you even want to see it or is now not the time?

**If yes:** Send page.

## OBJECTION HANDLING — NEPQ

### "How much is it?"
> General's $1K, VIP's $2.5K, Deploy tier's $5K. But honestly the real question isn't price — it's whether the math works for YOUR business. What are you doing monthly right now? *(let them answer, then show the ROI math)*

### "I don't have time for a 3-day event."
> Totally fair. If I'm being honest though, what's costing you more time right now — 3 days to install AI across your business, or another 6 months of doing it the old way? No wrong answer, just curious how you're weighing it.

### "I need to think about it."
> Yeah of course. What specifically do you want to think through? Sometimes I can save people the research.

### "Can I bring my partner / team?"
> VIP tier includes a +1. General is solo. Deploy tier we'll actually install it on both of your machines so you go home with it running.

### "What if I can't make it?"
> Transferable to a partner, not refundable. We're capping 20 seats because we're building on everyone's business live — can't replace you last minute.

### "Why should I trust you guys over [competitor]?"
> You shouldn't yet. Look at mynewstaff.ai/proof and mynewstaff.ai/results, watch the videos, then decide. If you're still on the fence I'll send you one of my operator clients and you can ask them directly — no script.

## THE GOLDEN RULE
**Never sell in the DM. Get them curious → send the page → let the page sell.**

Every time you feel tempted to paste features into a DM, stop and ask another question instead.
