**Offer to influencer:**
> You post about MNS / the intensive 2x a month. In return, you get:
> 1. Free VIP seat at every intensive for the next 12 months.
> 2. Branded deploy package — MNS installs our stack on YOUR business, free.
> 3. 20% commission on every attendee you refer.
> 4. "Official Partner" badge + mentions in all our content.
>
> We don't pay you in cash. We pay you in infrastructure that makes your business worth more.

**Target:** 5 JV partners in 90 days. Each one brings 1–2 attendees per event = free marketing for life.

**Math:** 5 partners × 2 attendees × $1,500 avg ticket × 20% commission = $3,000 payout. But the machine runs every event. Perpetual warm pipeline.

## THE BIG PICTURE

Revenue from the 20-seat event alone isn't the goal. The goal is:

- Warm pipeline for $50K+ deploy deals (1–2 closes = $50K–$100K)
- Warm pipeline for $12K/yr mastermind (6 closes = $72K ARR)
- Case studies and social proof for the next cohort
- JV partners who become permanent marketing machines
- Long-term equity opportunities with top operators

**Realistic 90-day projection from one event:**
- Event revenue: $37K
- Deploy deals (2): $75K
- Mastermind (6): $72K
- JV-sourced next-cohort: $30K more revenue
- **Total: ~$214K from one 3-day event**

**Rule:** Never price the event based on what the event is worth. Price it based on how many right people you need in the room to make the back-end pay.
