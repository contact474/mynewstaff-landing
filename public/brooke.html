FAQ

Does Brooke actually have a visible face on calls? +
Yes. Brooke appears as a video participant with a moving, lip-synced face. She looks like a real person on camera. Prospects don't know they're talking to AI unless you tell them.
What platforms does Brooke support? +
Zoom, Google Meet, and Microsoft Teams. Any platform that accepts a meeting join link. Webex and custom SIP coming Q2 2026.
How does Brooke handle objections? +
Brooke is trained on the NEPQ (Neuro-Emotional Persuasion Questioning) framework. She doesn't push or pitch. She asks calibrated questions that let the prospect sell themselves. When objections come up, she acknowledges, probes deeper, and reframes -- exactly how a top 1% closer would.
Can I customize Brooke's persona? +
On Growth and Scale plans, you build custom personas. Define her name, voice, personality, product knowledge, qualification criteria, and follow-up behavior. She becomes your employee -- with your company's knowledge and your sales playbook.
What happens after the call ends? +
Full recording, transcript, and AI-generated summary with qualification score and recommended next steps. On Growth+, this syncs directly to your CRM. Brooke can also send a personalized follow-up email within 60 seconds of hanging up.
Is this legal and compliant? +
Brooke operates within standard video call recording consent laws. She identifies as an AI assistant at the start of each call (configurable). You're responsible for compliance with local recording consent regulations, same as any call recording tool.
What's the real cost per call? +
Underlying cost is $0.04/minute. On Starter ($997/mo for 30 calls), that averages about $33/call for a 15-minute conversation. Your SDR costs $4,500/mo and makes maybe 8 quality calls a day. The math isn't close.
Can Brooke handle multiple calls at once? +
On Scale, Brooke can run 10+ calls simultaneously. Each instance is fully independent with its own context. Your bottleneck stops being headcount.
