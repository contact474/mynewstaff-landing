Questions

FAQ

Does Brooke actually have a visible face on video calls? +
Yes. On the Video Avatar plans, Brooke appears as a real video participant with a moving, lip-synced face powered by HeyGen. She looks like a real person on camera. Your prospect sees a face, hears a voice, and has a natural conversation. On Voice Agent plans, she works as a phone-style AI — no video needed.
How does the per-minute pricing work? +
Each plan includes a set number of minutes. Voice minutes cover cold calls, inbound, and phone conversations. Avatar minutes cover video calls on Zoom/Meet/Teams. You only pay for actual talk time — no charges while on hold or during silence. Go over your plan? Overage rates are listed on each tier. No surprises.
What does "computer vision" mean in practice? +
Brooke can see the other participants through the meeting camera feed. She reads facial expressions, body language, and engagement signals. If someone looks confused, she slows down. If they're nodding, she moves to close. It's the same thing a great salesperson does instinctively — except Brooke never misses a cue.
How does Brooke compare to Bland, Vapi, or other AI callers? +
Voice-only tools like Bland ($0.09/min), Vapi ($0.10-$0.30/min depending on stack), and Retell ($0.07-$0.25/min) are solid for basic outbound calling. Brooke's voice agent is priced competitively at $0.25/min. The difference: NEPQ sales methodology built-in (not generic scripts you write yourself), plus the option to add a video avatar on Zoom — something no voice-only platform offers at all.
How does Brooke handle objections? +
Brooke is trained on NEPQ (Neuro-Emotional Persuasion Questioning). She doesn't push or pitch. She asks calibrated questions that let the prospect sell themselves. When objections come up, she acknowledges, probes deeper, and reframes — exactly how a top 1% closer would. And she gets better at it with every call through her learning loop.
What platforms does Brooke work on? +
Video Avatar: Zoom, Google Meet, and Microsoft Teams — any platform that accepts a meeting join link. Voice Agent: works over phone lines and WebSocket connections. On-Page Widget: embeds directly on your website — Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge. No SDK or IT department needed.
What happens after the call ends? +
You get a full recording, transcript, and AI-generated summary with a qualification score and recommended next steps. On Growth/Business plans and above, this syncs directly to your CRM. Brooke can also send a personalized follow-up email within 60 seconds of hanging up.
Is this legal and compliant? +
Brooke identifies as an AI assistant at the start of each call (configurable). She operates within standard video call recording consent laws. You're responsible for compliance with local regulations, same as any call recording tool. We provide consent language templates for all major jurisdictions.
